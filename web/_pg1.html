Causa — Playground

Statement viewer

Throw agent logs and outcome exports at the engine — as many of each as you like, in whatever shape they come — and it triangulates: which log joins to which outcomes, what each agent actually produced, what every unit of output costs, and which levers would buy you more. Everything runs in your browser; nothing is uploaded anywhere.

Lever 2 · mix

Different inputs?

Observed data can't split segments yet — the engine measures a mix change from evidence you record: a model switch, a routing change, a staged rollout. Any one of them becomes Grade B evidence.
